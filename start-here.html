Start Here (10 minutes)

This is your simple setup to avoid confusion and start posting consistently.

  1. Pick one platform: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or LinkedIn.
  2. Pick one goal: grow audience, build trust, get clients, or sell a product.
  3. Pick one niche: fitness, food, education, beauty, business, etc.
  4. Post 3 times this week: one educational, one story, one simple tip.
  5. Repeat weekly: improve one thing at a time (hook, visuals, or consistency).

Rule: Consistency beats perfection.
